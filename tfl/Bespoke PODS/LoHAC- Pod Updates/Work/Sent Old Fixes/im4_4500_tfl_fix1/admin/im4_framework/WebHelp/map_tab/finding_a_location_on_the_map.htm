Finding a location on the map

The map tab has a dedicated search dialog with type ahead. Start to type the name of a street, street, group or place and you will be presented with a selection of results that meet your criteria

Click on your search selection and the search field will be updated then click [Search Map] and the map will zoom to your selected feature which will be highlighted as below

Active Overview Map

The overview map appears by default in the bottom right corner of the map. It helps to orientate you by showing the location of the current map view in the context of a larger geographical area. The area currently displayed in the map is shown as a red box in the overview map. At any time, click “minimise” to hide the overview map or click “expand” to display it again.

Drag and drop the red box in the overview map

Drag and drop the area outside the red box to pan the map
