Pod Controls

Dragging, opening and closing Pods

Pods can be dragged around the tab and positioned in one of the three virtual columns. Drag the Pod by clicking within its title bar, a set of placement guides will indicate the position of the pod when released, see

Figure 5

Figure 5 Repositioning Pods

 

Pods can be closed by clicking on the button within the Pod title bar and when closed can be opened by clicking on the button. The positions and open/closed status of Pods are saved when a user logs out of Information Manager.
